Future of ValidoAI System

Look into the Future of ValidoAI

We are working on enhancing the ValidoAI system to provide users with even more precise financial data analysis and better predictions. These features are in development and planned for future system versions.

AI Liquidity Analysis

We plan to enable automatic assessment of cash flows, debts, and financial obligations to predict potential liquidity issues. The system will use analyses based on Basel III standards to help users avoid financial bottlenecks and make better decisions.

Technologies: Machine learning for time series, statistical prediction, Basel III standard-based financial models.

AI Anomaly Detection

Advanced algorithms will be able to recognize suspicious transactions, deviations from standard patterns, and potential financial fraud. The system will use machine learning to reduce false alarms and precisely mark transactions requiring additional verification.

Technologies: AI models for anomaly detection, unsupervised machine learning, NLP invoice analysis, big data transaction analysis.

Macroeconomic Data

ValidoAI will enable synchronization with market data, exchange rates, and industry averages to make analyses even more precise. Users will be able to track economic indicators and adjust their business strategies according to market trends.

Technologies: Web scraping for data from central banks, AI models for trend analysis, API integration with financial institutions.

IFRS 9 & Basel III

The system will enable automatic assessment of customer credit ratings, helping users avoid bad debt risks. Algorithms will analyze customer data and tax reports to identify potential financial risks and enable better tax planning.

Technologies: Web scraping of tax regulations, AI chatbot for automatic tax question responses, document parsing and regulation analysis.
